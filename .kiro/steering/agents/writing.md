# Writing — the communication craft

Loadable by ANY role when authoring a piece meant to land with an audience — brief, manifesto, pitch deck, one-pager, essay, explanatory doc, speech, video script, email, announcement. Owns HOW it communicates (idea-force, narrative arc, segmentation, tone, impact principles), never the domain content. Load BEFORE drafting.
